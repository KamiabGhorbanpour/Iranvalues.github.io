Iranvalues

Iranvalues is a political values quiz designed to map opinions across multiple ideological spectra relevant to Iranian politics, society, and governance. You will be presented with a series of statements and asked to respond from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree.


There are questions in total.



How it works

Your answers influence several independent ideological axes, each represented as a spectrum rather than a binary choice. At the end of the quiz, you will see where your views fall along each spectrum.


Notes

This project is experimental and exploratory. Results are not definitive labels, but visual summaries of expressed preferences based on your answers.
